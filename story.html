From product leadership
to leadership mentoring

Blending product discipline with human-centered leadership.

I spent years in digital product environments where decisions are rarely clean—and leadership shows up when the pressure is real: stakeholders misaligned, priorities competing, teams tired, and outcomes still expected.

Early in my career, I built a foundation in structured problem solving, product thinking, cross-functional alignment and agile delivery. Over time that foundation became a framework for helping leaders navigate complexity with clarity and confidence.

Today I mentor leaders and teams to cut through the noise, make better decisions, and build momentum without burning people out. My edge is product thinking applied to leadership: structured, pragmatic, and human–centered.

I work globally with executives, product organizations and high-stakes teams to foster clarity, strengthen decision cycles, and create sustainable growth patterns. It’s not just about what gets shipped — it’s about how people work together to get there.
