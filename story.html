My story, in short

The pattern I kept seeing: teams don’t fail because they lack talent. They fail because they lack clarity — on goals, trade-offs, ownership, decision-making and pace.

Today, I help leaders turn complexity into direction: define what matters, decide faster, communicate better, and move forward with a sustainable rhythm.
