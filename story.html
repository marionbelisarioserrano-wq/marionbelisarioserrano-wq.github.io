My story, in short

I spent years in product and digital environments where the work is rarely “clean”: competing priorities, multiple stakeholders, and high expectations with limited time. In those contexts, leadership isn’t a title — it’s a daily practice.

Over time, I realized something: many teams don’t fail because they lack talent. They fail because they lack clarity — on goals, ownership, decision-making and pace.

Today, I work with leaders and teams to turn complexity into direction: define what matters, decide faster, communicate better, and move with intent. My edge is combining product thinking with leadership mentoring: pragmatic, structured, and deeply human.

If you’re navigating growth, change, or high-stakes delivery and you want a calmer, clearer way forward — let’s talk.
