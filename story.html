My story

My story, in short

I grew in product and digital environments where work is rarely “clean”: competing priorities, complex stakeholders, high expectations and limited time. In those contexts, leadership isn’t a title — it’s a daily practice.

Over time, I noticed a pattern: teams don’t fail because they lack talent. They fail because they lack clarity — on goals, trade-offs, ownership, decision-making and pace.

Clarity: what matters now — and what doesn’t.
Decisions: faster cycles, fewer loops, better trade-offs.
Execution: momentum without burnout.

Today, I help leaders turn complexity into direction: define priorities, decide with confidence, communicate with intent, and move forward with a sustainable rhythm.
