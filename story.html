My story

My story, in short

I grew in product and digital environments where the work is rarely “clean”: competing priorities, multiple stakeholders, high expectations, limited time. In those contexts, leadership isn’t a title — it’s a daily practice.

Over time, I noticed a pattern: teams don’t fail because they lack talent. They fail because they lack clarity — on goals, trade-offs, ownership, decision-making and pace.

Today, I help leaders turn complexity into direction: define what matters, decide faster, communicate better, and move with intent — while protecting energy and performance over time.
